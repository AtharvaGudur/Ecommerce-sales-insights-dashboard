Project Description:
This project presents a comprehensive retail analytics solution developed using Power BI, aimed at analyzing customer behavior, sales performance, and overall business trends.

The dashboard transforms raw transactional data into meaningful insights through interactive visualizations and KPIs, enabling stakeholders to make data-driven business decisions. It focuses on identifying patterns in customer purchases, product performance, regional sales, and revenue trends.

🎯 Objective
The primary objective of this project is to:
Analyze customer purchasing behavior
Track sales and revenue performance
Identify high-performing products and regions
Measure customer retention and repeat purchases
Provide actionable insights to improve business strategy and profitability

Insights:

Monthly revenue trends highlight seasonal fluctuations

📈 Business Insights

📌 High customer retention with a significant number of repeat customers

📌 Office Supplies is the top-performing category

📌 West region leads in total sales and revenue

📌 Sales trends show seasonal peaks during certain months

📌 Majority of orders are shipped via Standard Class

📌 A small set of products contributes to a large portion of revenue (Pareto principle)

Dimension Tables:

Products

Stores

Returns

Relationships are established using Primary Keys and Foreign Keys to ensure data integrity and optimized performance.
